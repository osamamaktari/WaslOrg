Wasl for peace orgnaziation

is a youth-led, non-governmental, independent, and non-profit organization that works in the fields of peacebuilding and youth empowerment to contribute effectively to achieving sustainable development. It was established as a youth initiative in 2013 with the aim of promoting peacebuilding within the community. Over time, its activities expanded to include multiple areas. In 2020, it transitioned from a youth initiative to an official youth organization under license number 34. The organization operates across the Republic of Yemen, with its headquarters located in the city of Taiz.

Our Vision

Leading locally in delivering youth programs and peacebuilding initiatives to effectively contribute to achieving sustainable development.
